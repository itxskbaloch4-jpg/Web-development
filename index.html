Our Story

Almost Three Decades of Digital Excellence

Founded in 1997, Gunn Interactive has grown from a small Dunedin studio into one of New Zealand's most trusted web agencies. We've watched the web evolve from static pages to dynamic applications — and we've led the way at every step.


Our philosophy is simple: every business deserves a website that works as hard as they do. Based at 96 London St, Dunedin, we bring local knowledge and global standards to every project.
